Vesta Trade

Welcome to the real world. A place where people are on tight deadlines, people forget things and people need everything... Yesterday.

Thats why we invented Vesta Trade

Vesta Trade can be bolted on to any vesta store or is available as a stand alone product and allows your customers to place orders via their smartphone or tablet anytime, anywhere. Customers can:

  • place orders 24 hours a day with a secure login
  • browse all the products you have to offer
  • view and organise their current orders
  • Create repeat orders and favourite lists
  • track their order status
  • receive notifications once the order is ready to pickup or has been delivered

Vesta trade makes it easier for your customer and that will help increase sales for your business. Vesta trade will suit businesses that want to sell online but only want to focus on their trade customer base.

  • Maximum Sales (p.a) – UNLIMITED
  • Mobile Responsive
  • Discounting Rules Engine
  • Abandoned Cart Saver
  • Customer Login Section
  • Customer Groups

  • Price Rules for Customer Groups
